SCREEN RECORDING PERMISSION

macOS blocks screen capture until you allow it: System Settings → Privacy & Security → Screen Recording → enable this app, then fully quit and relaunch (the toggle does nothing until restart).

Heads-up: macOS 15 Sequoia re-asks roughly monthly — that's Apple, not us.
