WELCOME

Circle it. Get an answer.

Your screen is frozen. Draw around anything — an error, a chart, a line — and the answer streams onto a sticky note right beside it.

  • 1Circle something with the marker.
  • 2Type a question, or just press Enter to let it infer one.
  • 3Read the note. Drag it, save it with ⤓, or dismiss with ✕.

Ctrl/⌘+Shift+D draw · Esc dismiss · Ctrl/⌘+Shift+Q quit

ADD YOUR API KEY

Answers come from Groq's free tier. Grab a key at console.groq.com/keys (no card required) and paste it below — it's stored locally on this machine. Or quit and relaunch with GROQ_API_KEY set.

MACOS PERMISSION

Allow screen capture

macOS blocks screen capture until you grant it. In System Settings → Privacy & Security → Screen & System Audio Recording:

  • 1Add the app that launched Draw to Ask — your Terminal (or iTerm) if you ran drawtoask, or Draw to Ask for a packaged build.
  • 2Turn its toggle on.
  • 3Fully quit and relaunch — the toggle does nothing until you restart.

Heads-up: macOS 15 Sequoia re-asks roughly monthly — that's Apple, not us.
